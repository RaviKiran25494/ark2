Why Ark Cloud City?

Ark Cloud City is a residential development by Ark Group. Easy functionality and smart aesthetics combine at Ark Cloud city to offer the ultimate home experience. Ark Cloud city is located in Whitefield, Bangalore. The region is considered one of the most favoured areas for living in Bangalore. Those who prefer to have a home and work close to each other will find that the neighbourhood is ideal.

Ark Cloud City is a gated community with a total area of 2.18 acres and has a safer environment due to its compact design. The community has five wings all G+4. This gated community in Bangalore is home to 130+ happy families and has apartments that are available for immediate occupancy.

Living the good life
Video Door Phone

Providing an exceptional experience for our residents, Ark Cloud City, Whitefield, utilizes the latest technologies. Every apartment comes with a video door phone for verifying visitor identity. As a result, anyone staying home alone feels safe.

Temperature-Controlled Swimming Pool

Consider going swimming on a cloudy, windy day. It's impossible, isn't it? Yet Ark Cloud City, Whitefield is proving otherwise. Since we have a temperature-controlled swimming pool, residents can swim at any time of the day.

WI-FI Ready Apartments

Ark Cloud City, Whitefield offers Wi-Fi in every home. Rekindle joy. Make sharing fun! When needed, the Wi-Fi needed to be activated. While you watch a movie with your children, you can download the latest bestseller on your Kindle.

Sensor-Based Lights For Bathrooms

Ark Cloud City, Whitefield has sensor lights in the bathrooms. No more fumbling with switches for the elderly or visitors. Additionally, the bathroom sensor will turn itself off as soon as you leave, saving you money. Perfect, right?

Video Surveillance

Reveal all that has ever happened. At Ark Cloud City, Whitefield Life now has a rewind button. A video camera is watching over your kids when they play and you can reconfirm who visited your apartment complex. This way, you stay in total control of your home environment.

Smart Lighting Systems

Access your living room lights from your smartphone. From your smartphone or tablet, you can now control the lights and fans. When dusk falls, you simply turn on the study room lights. Their eyes sparkle as they wait for you to return home.

Club House

With access to our clubhouse, we have simplified the socialization process, you will be able to spend more time doing what you enjoy. You can participate in a wide variety of clubhouse activities to satisfy your interests.

Jogging Track

To stay in shape and to keep themselves refreshed, members of the community can make use of the jogging track in the community. Running on our jogging tracks will allow you to enjoy the fresh air while you work up a sweat.

Kids Play Area

Greenery covers the area all year round and at all times of the year. The kids-play area lets children engage in fun and learning activities while reducing screen consumption and reducing their time spent watching television.

Specifications

Structure

RCC framed structure.

Super Structure

Cement concrete block masonry.

Plastering

All internal and external walls with smooth plaster finish.

Doors

Main door in teak frame with molded skin door shutter and internal doors in sal wood frame with molded skin door shutter.

Windows

Powder-coated/ Anodised aluminium windows with MS safety grills.

Painting

All internal walls with the putty finish and two coats of emulsion paint over a coat of primer. External walls with two coats of exterior emulsion paint of Asian or equivalent make, over a coat of primer.

Flooring

Hall, dining, bedrooms and kitchen with vitrified tiles of good quality (size 24" x 24"). Ceramic tile flooring in bathrooms, balcony & utility (size 12" x 12")

Kitchen

Granite platform with stainless steel sink.

Plumbing & Sanitaryware

CP fittings of Jaguar or equivalent make in toilets and kitchen, Parryware or equivalent make sanitaryware in all toilets.

Electrical

Concealed copper wire of Anchor or equivalent make. Modular Switches: MK or equivalent make TV telephone points in living and all bedrooms and geyser points in all bathrooms.

Lifts

6 passenger capacity lifts of Johnson or equivalent make

Sewerage Treatment Plant

A sewerage treatment plant will be provided to treat the waste water

Power Backup

Power backup for common lighting, lifts and 100% Power backup for all lights and fans.

Dadoing Kitchen

Glazed ceramic tiles dado up to 2 ft. height above kitchen platform

Dadoing Toilets

Glazed ceramic tile (size: 12" x 18") dado up to 7 ft. in height
