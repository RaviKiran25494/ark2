Our Success Story

Ark was founded in the year 1989 with a defined set of values that translate into Openness, Accountability, Trust and Happiness, in all areas of business.

At the inception of Ark, we took the OATH to build solid structures that provide long-lasting value to our clients and now, we’re extending our OATH to reach 5000 crores by 2030.

Ark has a track record of pushing its boundaries and venturing out of the box. From a CEO’s home to multi-storied complexes, corporate structures to communities, Ark has ventured into all zones.

The reason we have tasted success is that we have a clear set of organizational and personal goals which are interdependent. We don’t stop at just setting the goals, we pursue them passionately.

30+ years of delivering exclusivity in form, function and aesthetics, reinforced by the commitment to timely delivery.
