Why Ark Aptha?

This residential project comprises 68 units of 2BHK and 3BHK apartments with thoughtful amenities and landscaping designed to enhance the quality of living. With an Ark Aptha home, you are not just investing in the physical space, but you also become part of the trust and legacy Ark Group has built over the years. Ark Aptha offers apartments that offer true value, peace of mind, and a sense of home, but there are only a limited number of them available.

Living the good life
A/C Gymnasium

We have a gym that everyone can use to stay fit and in shape. The equipment in the gym is regularly maintained periodically.

Car parking

Why Karmanghat?

Karmanghat is a residential neighbourhood in east Hyderabad. The nearest railway station is Yakutpura, which is 6km away. Three kilometres away is the LB Nagar metro station along the red line of the Hyderabad metro. There is an easy connection to the rest of Hyderabad via NH 65.
