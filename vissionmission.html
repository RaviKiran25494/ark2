Vission & Mission

Mission

Slow and steady has been the mission, vision and philosophy of Ark. In a supersonic world where everything is defined by high speed, Ark has become the flag bearer of deep foundations and carefully measured growth. The teams have been built consciously.

Values have been ingrained and percolated across all departments. With a strong workforce of women, Ark is a universe of equal opportunities. With 80% of the staff being under 40, it’s a young progressive organisation quite literally, being headed by a 25 year old woman CEO and an experienced woman COO. With a winning combination of staff in the age range of 50 to 25, Ark is an organisation where wisdom meets innovation.

Ark takes inspiration from the Oak tree that outlives its contemporaries does so because of its deep and long-reaching widespread roots. Here are a few inspiring facts about the majestically tall and deep Oak:
