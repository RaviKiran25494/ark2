Ark Foundation

Step towards a better future

From facilitating better learning environments for the needy to touching hundreds of farmer lives through the art of poetry, Ark Foundation has gone above and beyond to serve society and make the world a better place.

The foundation’s vision percolates deep in the organisation’s roots, which is reflected by the fact that all the employees of Ark Group have pledged for organ donation. Also, societal impact initiatives are also taken up internally by helping employees and their families for better education, lifestyle and providing an environment that supports growth.

Going above future and beyond

Ark Foundation strongly believes that there should be equal growth and learning opportunities for every part of society. Going above and beyond its CSR mandate, Ark Foundation encourages and facilitates financial aid to students. Among other ways, Ark Foundation gives back to society by:

Helping underprivileged students with financial aid

Providing infrastructure facilities in government schools

Supporting living conditions for a better learning environment

Promoting organ donation

All deserve a better life.

  • Donations are made to schools for children with special needs.
  • Distribution of medicines to the needy.
  • Distribution of bicycles, shoes, laptops and other necessities to underprivileged, meritorious students.
  • Constructing a room for Chaitanya Seva Ashramam, Boorgula Village, Mahbubnagar.
  • Sponsored a room for Reddy’s Girls Hostel at Suryapet, Andhra Pradesh.
  • Donated land for construction of a school in Nalgonda Dist.
  • Adopted a school at Lakshmapuram Village, Ramannapet, Telangana.
  • Supports Ananda Vasathi Gruham, an orphanage that provides shelter to homeless children.
  • Conduct regular motivational speech camps for students at various government schools to reduce school drop-outs.

Enabling Talent

Success needs more than just talent sometimes. Ark Foundation provided all the necessary financial aid to Aditya (a star baseball player), to go overseas and win accolades for the country.

Motivating the Gen-next

Ark Foundation organises various Motivational Speech Camps in Government Schools to reduce school drop-outs, encourage students to become aspiring leaders of tomorrow.
