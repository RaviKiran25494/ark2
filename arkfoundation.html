Ark Foundation

Step towards a better future

Ark Foundation has gone above and beyond to help society and make the world a better place by facilitating educational opportunities for the needy. Moreover, Ark Group employees have pledged their organs for donation, meaning the foundation's vision is deeply embedded in the roots of the company. As part of our social impact policies, we also provide assistance to employees and their families in the areas of education, lifestyle and work environment.

Going above future and beyond

Ark Foundation strongly believes that there should be equal growth and learning opportunities for every part of society. Going above and beyond its CSR mandate, Ark Foundation encourages and facilitates financial aid to students. Among other ways, Ark Foundation gives back to society by:

Helping underprivileged students with financial aid

Providing infrastructure facilities in government schools

Supporting living conditions for a better learning environment

Promoting organ donation
